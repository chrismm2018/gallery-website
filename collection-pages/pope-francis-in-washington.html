"Pope Francis in Manhattan"
 Pope Francis visited Manhattan in 2016.  The National Museum of Catholic Art & Library, formerly of New York is planning a museum in DC.  They exhibit other Gatto works and asked him to make depictions of the Pope in New York, at the Capitol in DC and at the National Cathedral. Portrait is rendered in pastels on fine paper.
